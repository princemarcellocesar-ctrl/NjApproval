NEWARK CITY HALL PERMIT ROUTING SECRETS:

1. THE STARTING LINE (ROOM 412): 
All commercial projects must start at the Engineering Department in Room 412. You must get your "Engineering Site Plan Review" and "Water/Sewer Connection" approvals here BEFORE you can file for a building permit.

2. THE "DEAL-KILLER" CHECK (ROOM B1):
Code Enforcement is in Room B1. If you are selling a property or changing its use, you need a "C.O. Inspection" from this room.

3. THE FILING DESK (ROOM B23):
The Building Division / UCC (Uniform Construction Code) is in Room B23. This is where you hand in your F100 and Subcode forms. 
SECRET: They will REJECT your application if you don't have the "Water/Sewer Receipt" from Room 412 first.

4. THE TAX TRAP:
You must prove your property taxes are paid up to date at the Tax Collector's office before the Building Division will issue a final permit.
